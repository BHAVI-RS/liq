About

HORDEX is a decentralized liquidity providing platform built on Uniswap V2. Users put in funds into liquidity pools of newly launched tokens to generate LP positions in curated token pools, earn passive returns as liquidity providers, and grow their network through a multi-level referral commission system.

LP positions are time-locked to ensure platform stability, then claimable or removable once the lock period expires. Referral commissions are distributed automatically across up to 10 levels of your network on every investment made by your referrals.

How Does It Work
1
Register — Connect your wallet and sign up under a referral link to join the HORDEX network.
2
Invest ETH — Choose a token pool and invest. 50% adds liquidity on Uniswap V2, 30% buys pool tokens, and 20% is distributed as referral commissions up to 10 levels deep.
3
Earn LP Fees — Your LP position earns Uniswap trading fees and accrues value as the pool grows during the lock period.
4
Claim LP Tokens — After the lock period expires, claim your LP tokens directly to your wallet.
5
Remove Liquidity — Return LP tokens to convert your position back to ETH and tokens at any time after claiming.
6
Refer & Earn — Share your referral link. Earn automatic commissions on every investment your referrals make — across 10 levels.

FAQ
HORDEX is a decentralized liquidity platform on Uniswap V2. You invest ETH, which is split — half buys project tokens from the pool and pairs with ETH as LP, and half funds your referral commission chain. After the lock period you can claim your LP tokens or remove liquidity to receive ETH and tokens back.
LP positions are locked for 1 minute on the current testnet deployment. Production lock durations will be longer to ensure pool stability. The exact unlock time is shown on each investment card in your dashboard.
Every investment distributes 20% of the invested ETH across up to 10 levels of the referrer chain. Level 1 earns 10%, Level 2 earns 5%, Level 3 earns 2%, and so on. Commissions are paid instantly on-chain to each eligible referrer's wallet.
Yes. You must register with a referrer's wallet address before you can invest. The owner's wallet is the root referrer — if you don't have a referral link, contact the platform admin for a registration address.
The smart contract is currently in testnet phase. A full third-party security audit is planned before mainnet deployment. All contract code is open-source and verifiable on-chain.
When you remove liquidity, the contract pulls your LP tokens, burns them via Uniswap, and sends you back a proportional share of both ETH and project tokens from the pool. The amounts depend on the pool ratio at the time of removal.
