The amounts are distributed after deducting the platform fee of 5% from the commissions. Ineligible levels are skipped — commission passes upward to the next eligible upline.
ELIGIBILITY RULES
◆Must hold an active LP position to receive commissions at any level.
◆Level N requires at least N active direct referrals, each holding their own investment.
◆If no eligible person exists at a level, the share climbs upline until claimed or falls to the platform.
COMMISSION CAP — 5× PACKAGE VALUE PER POSITION
Each LP position can earn referral commissions totalling up to 5× its original package value. Once the cap is hit, that position is ✕ INELIGIBLE and skipped for commissions until new investment of any amount is done.

Commission Status
● ELIGIBLE Has atleast one active LP position locked, referral cap not reached and meets the minimum direct referral count for that level. Commission is paid immediately on each downline investment.
✕ INELIGIBLE No active LP position, or the 5× commission cap for that position has been reached. Commissions are skipped and passed to the next eligible upline. To become eligible, an active investment is to be locked.
⏸ PAUSED Active LP position exists, referral cap not reached, but investment is not locked. Commissions resume only after restaking that position.
