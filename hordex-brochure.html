HOW ROI COMMISSIONS WORK
◆Each time a downline investor locks an LP position, a stream is assigned to each eligible upline level for the lock's full duration.
◆Each stream earns a share of the downline's staking reward — accruing every second, proportional to time elapsed in their lock.
◆When the downline restakes, the old stream settles and a new one begins — ROI commissions always reflect the active lock and its current rate.
